SCOPE

This Privacy Policy applies to TestGang customers and TestGang.net visitors, unless a distinct privacy policy is disclosed. TestGang.com is the online service of TestGang (Collectively “we,” or “us”). In this statement you will find TestGang information practices regarding the personal information you provide to us by electronic means. That is to say, how personal information collected online is stored and processed by TestGang. Please read it carefully, as, by using this website, you acknowledge that you have read and accept our privacy processes.

WHAT PERSONAL INFORMATION IS COLLECTED

TestGang may collect information provided by you when you use our online service TestGang.net, and when you correspond with us by email and social media.
In particular, the personal information or personal data (expression used by the Swedish Personal Data Act) that TestGang may collect and process are:
  • Email address
  • Internet protocol (IP) address
  • Your gadget type
  • Your gadget’s Operating System
Under the Swedish Personal Data Act “personal data” refers to any type of information that directly or indirectly identifies a living natural person.

HOW PERSONAL INFORMATION IS COLLECTED

The personal information detailed above may be collected:
  • By filling in TestGang online form
  • Through email when you communicate to us by this mean of communication
  • Statistical and analytical information is collected through HTML5 Web Storage. The operating system on your computer will provide tools to the applications for storing and retrieving data. This is information can be stored in .INI files, preference files, XML files, and some other places depending upon your operating system and the application you're using.

HOW PERSONAL INFORMATION IS USED

We collect and process your information fairly and lawfully. This information is collected for specific, explicit, and legitimate purposes and not further processed in a way incompatible with such purposes.
We use the information we collect or receive from you to:
  • Improve the access and usability of our services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send you periodic emails providing you with information about products and services
  • Communicate with you and answer your questions
  • Administering orders and accounts relating to our customers
  • For statistical analysis and systems testing but the identity of members and site visitors will be protected at all times
We keep personal information about you for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Statement, unless a longer retention period is required by applicable law. Archiving of certain financial information is done pursuant to the applicable accounting legislation, in our case the Swedish Bookkeeping Act (1999:1078), the Annual Accounts Act (1995:1554).

We, therefore, and in regard to that, leave it to your own discretion the use of this website by you, as well as the process and maintenance of your personal information by us.

SOCIAL MEDIA

Google+
TestGang's website includes a Google+ button, your browser will directly connect to Google's servers and load the TestGang's Google+ account content.
TestGang will never know if and when you clicked the Google+ button. Google only provides us with aggregated geographic and demographic, non-identifiable statistics about the use of the Google+ button in our website.
Facebook Plugin
TestGang's website includes Facebook's plugin, your browser will connect directly to Facebook's servers and load the TestGang's Facebook account content. TestGang will never know if and when you clicked on the Facebook button.

To find out more about the data Google+ and Facebook collects for their own purposes and how such information is processed and used, please read their respective privacy policies: Google+ and Facebook.

Google has an ability to use and share this information and also it may also be used for targeted advertising into the websites. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser.
By using this website, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above.

HTML5 WEB STORAGE

HTML5 Web Storage is used to store data locally within the user's browser. It is more secure and faster than using cookies. The data is not included with every server request, but it is used ONLY when asked for. TestGang is using browser local storage for caching login information.
