ABOUT

SWAMPY LAW is focused on providing legal services to businesses and individuals within the European Union and through the world. My mission is to help clients with their legal issues by providing legal services in a professional manner. My expertise legal areas are: Internet, technology and digital law, Intellectual property, domestic and International business, commercial law, IT contracts, data protection, cloud computing.
My name is Vaida and I am young and enthusiastic entrepreneur. I have gained LLM in Law and Information Technology at Stockholm University and I am passionate about IT law. I speak fluently english and lithuanian, good russian and swedish language skills. I am also the co-founder and a member of an independent non-profit organization Organisation för Personlig Integritet och Dataskydd (OPID).
