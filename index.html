ABOUT US

KYPADO is a legal consultancy company that serves clients worldwide. Located in Stockholm, Sweden, Kypado has presence in several other countries (Lithuania, Spain, Canada, the US, and Italy). This network is continuously growing. In addition, we maintain affiliate relationships with professionals in other disciplines like ICT and engineering.

KYPADO has been established by Vaida Domeikyte and María Täng Palma, two lawyers with a multidisciplinary background and an international legal approach. We are a team of trusted highly skilled lawyers specialized in the fields of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Law, and Intellectual Property Rights (IPR).

At KYPADO, we continuously keep up-to-date of latest developments in the fields of law and technology. We are active members of both legal and technological associations, and regularly attend seminars and (ICT Law) events.

Our MISSION is to provide our clients with highly qualified, effective, and innovative consulting services in ICT Law and IPR.

Our VISION is to be a leading center for innovation of the legal services to businesses, government institutions, and individuals.
