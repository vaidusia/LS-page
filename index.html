Comprehensible & appealing multilayered legal notices

KYPADO develops visual legal content for websites and mobile applications. Our visual legal content consists of three layers. These three layers are needed if you want to be legally compliant while also being transparent and making sure your message can reach your entire target audience.


When using our layered documents you are:

  • Effortlessly complying with your legal obligations

  • Making sure your policies are understood by your target audience

  • Contributing to a more balanced contractual relationship and to website usability principles.


You are also giving your target audience what they want. People like information that is plain, distinct, graphically appealing, and easy to contrast. Our legal content is not only comprehensible, but also human centric, so that the people you deal with will easily grasp the meaning and enjoy the content.
